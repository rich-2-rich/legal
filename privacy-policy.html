Introduction

I Am Rich 2 ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of our application.

Information We Collect

I Am Rich 2 is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not store any data on our servers

Purchase Information

All in-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the collection and use of payment data. Your purchase history is managed by Apple and can be viewed in your App Store account.

Local Data Storage

The App may store your total membership value and membership tier status locally on your device. This information:

  • Remains only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any user data, there is no user data at risk. All app functionality occurs locally on your device.
